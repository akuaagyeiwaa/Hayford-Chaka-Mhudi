This project on colonization, religion and identity was inspired by my reading of a collection of works from one One More Voice This a plateform that hosts a lot of educative materials that habour on African studies.My project wi will will examine the role of colonization, religion and identity in a selection of texts. These includes Joseph Casely Hayford's Ethiopia unbound, Solomon Plaatje's Mhudi and Thomas Mofolo's Chaka. Together, these texts portaly the activities of European missionaries and trades as they lived and worked on various European colonies.
